# Our Mission

**BondCasts is the podcast app I always wanted - so I built it.**

Podcasts are one of the last truly open corners of the internet: an open feed,
a file, and you. BondCasts is built to keep it that way. It's a podcast player
that's simple enough to hand to anyone, deep enough to reward the people who
love tuning every detail, and honest about the fact that your listening is
yours - not a product to be packaged and sold.

## What we believe

- **Simple by default.** Open the app and press play. The things you do every
  day are one tap away; the complexity stays out of your way until you go
  looking for it.
- **Customizable to the core.** Rearrange your library, build smart playlists,
  fine-tune playback, and shape the app around how *you* listen - not how we
  assume you should.
- **Made for power users.** Chapters, transcripts, silence skipping, sync
  across your devices, Shortcuts, widgets, and more. The features enthusiasts
  ask for are the features we build.

## What we promise

- **We don't track you.** No accounts, no analytics, no profiles. Your
  subscriptions, history, and habits live on your devices and in your own
  private iCloud. The only thing our notification service ever sees is which
  public feeds want new-episode alerts - tied to a random ID, never to you.
- **We don't hold your data hostage.** Import and export your subscriptions as
  standard OPML any time. Your library is yours to take with you, always.
- **We don't show you ads.** No banners, no tracking pixels, no feed full of
  "recommended" noise. Just the shows you chose to follow.

BondCasts is a podcast app made by someone who loves podcasts, for people who
love podcasts. That's the whole idea.
