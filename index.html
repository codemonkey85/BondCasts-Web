Your podcasts, done right.

A fast, private podcast player for iPhone, iPad, and Mac — with iCloud sync, offline downloads, transcripts, and CarPlay.

Download on the App Store

Features

  • Syncs everywhere

    Your subscriptions, queue, and playback position stay in sync across all your devices with iCloud.

  • Listen offline

    Download episodes for the plane, the subway, or a dead zone, and auto-download new episodes with a simple toggle.

  • Transcripts & chapters

    Follow along with synced transcripts and jump straight to the chapters you care about.

  • Built for CarPlay

    Browse, queue, and play hands-free with a CarPlay interface designed for the road.

  • Smart playlists

    Build playlists that fill themselves based on rules you set — new episodes, unplayed, and more.

  • Private by design

    No tracking, no ads. Your listening data is yours and stays in your iCloud account.
