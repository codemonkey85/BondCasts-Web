Frequently asked

My subscriptions aren't syncing between devices.

Make sure both devices are signed in to the same iCloud account (the same Apple ID). Sync happens in the background over iCloud and can take a moment to catch up after a change.

How do I import my existing podcasts?

BondCasts supports OPML import. Export an OPML file from your current podcast app and import it from Settings.

Auto-downloads aren't happening.

In Settings, check that Auto-Download New Episodes (and, if you use it, auto-download for queued episodes) is turned on. On a device that's low on storage, make sure auto-downloads haven't been turned off for that device. Also confirm you have available storage for new downloads.
